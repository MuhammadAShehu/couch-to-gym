Couch-to-Gym Features

Workout and diet plans: When people start working out, they normally do not have the knowledge of how and what they should eat or which exact workouts to perform, with a workout and diet plan, there will be clear guidance regarding these important essentials. The diet plan is of high importance as a certain amount of protein needs to be consumed for optimum muscle growth and maintenance.


Types of workout plans: There are different types of workout plans. However, the main ones are; push and pull workout plans. The push workouts involve muscles that are used to push items, whilst, the pull workouts involve muscles that are used to pull items.


Gym classes: The gym classes will be coached and there will be instructors who can show the members of the club how to correctly perform workouts. The insturctors can also offer private sessions if needed, as everyone workouts out differently.
